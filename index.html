How It Works

Your Journey to
The Perfect Gown

01

Consultation

Book a session — in-person in Port Harcourt or virtual for international brides. We listen to your vision, wedding theme, silhouette preferences, and budget. No pressure, all love.

02

Design & Measurements

We take precise measurements and produce a custom design sketch. Every detail — fabric, beadwork, neckline, structure — is confirmed before we cut a single thread.

03

Crafting Your Gown

Our artisans hand-craft your gown with meticulous care. Each bead placed, each seam perfected. We send you progress updates at key milestones throughout.

04

Final Fitting & Delivery

A final fitting confirms perfect fit. Your gown is then delivered — locally or internationally — professionally packaged, tracked, and handled with care.

Got Questions?

Everything You
Need to Know

  • We recommend ordering at least 4–6 months before your wedding day for bespoke and custom gowns. This ensures enough time for design, crafting, fittings, and any final alterations. Rush orders may be available — contact us to discuss your timeline.

  • Bridal sizing runs larger than everyday fashion — a street size 10 can be a bridal size 14 or 16. For bespoke and custom gowns, we work entirely from your actual measurements, so the tag number is irrelevant. We provide a full measurement guide before you order.

  • Browse our rental collection, book a fitting, and reserve your gown with a deposit. Rentals are typically available for 3–5 days. A refundable damage deposit is required. Minor alterations can be arranged. Returns must be clean and undamaged.

  • Yes — we ship worldwide. Every gown is professionally packaged and sent with full tracking. Contact us for a delivery quote to your location.

  • Absolutely. We offer virtual consultations via video call for brides outside Port Harcourt and all international clients. Share inspiration images, we share design sketches and fabric options, and guide you through accurate measurements from home.

  • We require a 50% deposit to begin production, with the balance due before delivery. We accept bank transfer (Nigeria), international wire transfer, and mobile money. All payment terms are confirmed in writing before work begins.

  • Because every bespoke and custom gown is made to order for you, we do not offer refunds. We include one round of alterations at no extra cost in the final fitting stage. Significant changes requested after production begins may incur additional charges, discussed upfront.
